Our sessions are based on the Athletics Australia FIT model and long-term athlete development principles. We provide a fun, supportive and engaging environment where athletes can develop physical literacy, confidence and sport-specific skills at an age-appropriate pace.

Whether your child is taking their first steps in athletics or striving for performance goals, we have a squad to suit their stage of development.

## Which Session is Right for My Child?

| Squad                        | Age Guide            | Focus                                                    |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| FUNdamentals                 | Typically ages 5–8   | Movement skills, games and confidence                    |
| Athletic Allstars            | Typically ages 8–12  | Athletic skills and event foundations                    |
| Development & Track Squads   | Typically ages 10+   | Speed, endurance and technical development               |
| Performance & Teen Endurance | High school athletes | Performance-focused training and competition preparation |
