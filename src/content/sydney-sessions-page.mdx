Our sessions are based on the Athletics Australia FIT model training guidelines for long-term athlete development. We aim to provide a **fun** and **engaging** environment for children and teens to develop their **physical literacy** and **sport-specific skills**. These sessions are designed to be **age-appropriate** and **inclusive**, catering to the needs and abilities of each child.
