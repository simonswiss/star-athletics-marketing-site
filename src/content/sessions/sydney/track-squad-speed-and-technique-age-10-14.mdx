### Run faster, move smarter and compete with confidence

**Track Squad** focuses on developing speed, running technique and athletic movement skills in a fun and supportive environment. These sessions help athletes improve the mechanics that underpin performance across sprint, middle-distance and cross-country events.

Training places a greater emphasis on acceleration, speed development, coordination and running efficiency, helping athletes become stronger, faster and more confident movers.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Sprint mechanics, acceleration and stride efficiency
* Speed development for sprint and middle-distance events
* Agility, coordination and athletic movement skills
* Running technique and movement efficiency
* Confidence in training and competition
* A strong foundation for athletics and other sports

Sessions are structured to challenge athletes appropriately while maintaining a positive and engaging squad environment. Athletes are encouraged to challenge themselves, track their progress and enjoy the process of improvement.

**Suitable for:** Athletes aged 10+ looking to improve speed, technique and overall running performance.
