For high school athletes who love long-distance running (3k+) and want to improve for athletics, cross country, fun runs, or general fitness.

After a dynamic warm-up and basic drills, sessions focus on:

* Improving stamina and aerobic fitness
* Developing pacing and race strategy
* Increasing running efficiency and speed endurance

Join a supportive team of like-minded teens, push each other to improve, and enjoy the journey together. Start your day strong with a 6:30am session, training, having fun, and bringing out the best in each other!\
