### Building strong, confident and resilient distance runners

**Teen Endurance** is designed for high school athletes who enjoy long-distance running and want to improve their performance in athletics, cross country, fun runs and endurance events.

Following a dynamic warm-up and running drills, athletes complete structured training designed to progressively develop endurance, running efficiency and race performance in a supportive squad environment.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Aerobic fitness and endurance
* Running efficiency and technique
* Pacing, race strategy and tactical awareness
* Speed endurance and finishing strength
* Confidence, resilience and self-discipline
* A lifelong enjoyment of running and physical activity

Athletes train alongside a motivated group of like-minded runners who encourage and support one another to achieve their individual goals. Whether aiming for a personal best, preparing for cross country season or simply looking to become a stronger runner, Teen Endurance provides a positive and challenging environment to help athletes reach their potential.

**Suitable for:** High school athletes with an interest in distance running and endurance events.
