### Structured training for committed athletes

Our **Performance Squad** is designed for motivated high school athletes looking to take their speed, endurance and overall athletic performance to the next level. This squad provides advanced training that supports success in athletics, cross country and a wide range of field and team sports.

Athletes complete a shared warm-up before splitting into **speed** and **endurance** training streams to target event-specific performance.

**Speed athletes develop:**

* Acceleration and maximum velocity
* Sprint mechanics and technical efficiency
* Strength, power and athletic performance
* Race preparation and competition skills

**Endurance athletes develop:**

* Aerobic capacity and running efficiency
* Race pace awareness and tactical skills
* Speed endurance and finishing strength
* Competition readiness and resilience

With more challenging sessions, greater training expectations and a focus on long-term athletic development, this squad is ideal for athletes committed to achieving their performance goals within a supportive and motivated training environment.

**Suitable for:** High school athletes aged 13+.
