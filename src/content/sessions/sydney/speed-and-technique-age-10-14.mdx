For young athletes who want to run faster, move smarter, and compete with confidence. **Track Squad** focuses on building **speed**, refining running **technique**, and developing the athletic skills that make a difference on the track and in sport—including sprinting and middle-distance performance.

* Targeted drills to improve sprint mechanics, acceleration, and stride efficiency
* Speed development for sprinters and middle-distance athletes
* Agility, power, and athletic skills for track, field, and sport performance
* Structured, high-quality training in a supportive, fun environment
* Opportunities to challenge yourself, track progress, and enjoy competition
* Build confidence, teamwork, and a love of running
