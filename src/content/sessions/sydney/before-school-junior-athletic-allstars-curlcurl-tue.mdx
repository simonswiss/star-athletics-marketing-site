### Building athletic skills and confidence

**Junior Athletic Allstars** is designed for athletes in Years 3–4 who are ready to take the next step in their athletics journey. Sessions focus on developing athletics-specific skills while continuing to build confidence, coordination and overall athleticism in a fun and supportive environment.

Athletes are introduced to running, jumping and throwing techniques, sprint mechanics and basic race awareness through engaging activities that challenge them at an age-appropriate level.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Speed, agility and coordination
* Running, jumping and throwing skills
* Sprint mechanics and movement efficiency
* Confidence in training and competition
* Race awareness and athletic decision-making
* A strong foundation for future athletics development

Sessions are designed to be fun, engaging and challenging while helping athletes prepare for future participation in athletics, cross country and other sports.

**Suitable for:** Typically Years 3–4 (ages 8–10)
