### Developing athletic confidence and preparing for the next step

**Senior Athletic Allstars** is designed for athletes in Years 5–6 who are ready for greater challenge and more structured skill development. Sessions continue to build speed, agility and athleticism while introducing athletes to more advanced running, jumping and throwing techniques.

Athletes are encouraged to take ownership of their learning, challenge themselves and develop the confidence and skills needed to transition into Development Squad training.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Speed, agility and movement efficiency
* Athletics event foundations and technique
* Running, jumping and throwing skills
* Fitness, strength and athletic confidence
* Goal setting, resilience and self-belief
* Preparation for more structured athletics training

Sessions provide a fun and supportive environment while gradually introducing athletes to the expectations and skills required for the next stage of their athletics journey.

**Suitable for:** Typically Years 5–6 (ages 10–12).
