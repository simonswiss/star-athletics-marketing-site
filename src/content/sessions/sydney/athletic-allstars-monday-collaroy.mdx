### Help your child run faster, move better and build confidence

Athletics Allstars is designed for primary school athletes who want to develop athletic skills in a fun, engaging and supportive environment.

Our sessions focus on improving running technique, speed, agility, coordination and overall athleticism while helping children build confidence and enjoy being active. Athletes will learn fundamental athletics skills through a variety of games, drills and activities that keep training enjoyable and age-appropriate.

With experienced coaches leading the way, children are encouraged to challenge themselves, develop resilience and build skills that support not only athletics and cross country, but many other sports as well.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Speed, agility and coordination
* Running, jumping and throwing skills
* Improved fitness and movement efficiency
* Confidence and body awareness
* Athletics event foundations and technique

Sessions are adapted to suit the age, experience and stage of development of each athlete. We regularly differentiate activities and may split into smaller groups to ensure every child is appropriately challenged and supported.

**Suitable for:** Typically ages 8–12
