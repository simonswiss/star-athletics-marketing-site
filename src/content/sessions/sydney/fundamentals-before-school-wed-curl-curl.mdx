### Start the day active, confident and energised

Our **Before School FUNdamentals** session is designed for young children in Kindergarten to Year 2 who enjoy being active, playing games and developing movement skills in a fun and relaxed environment.

These sessions focus on building confidence, coordination and physical literacy through simple athletics-based activities, games and challenges. The emphasis is on participation, enjoyment and positive movement experiences to help children start their school day in the best possible way.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Fundamental movement skills (running, jumping and throwing)
* Coordination, balance and agility
* Confidence in movement and physical activity
* Social skills and teamwork
* A positive attitude towards sport and exercise

Sessions are designed to be fun, inclusive and engaging, helping children feel energised, confident and ready for the school day ahead.

**Suitable for:** Kindergarten–Year 2 (ages 5–8)
