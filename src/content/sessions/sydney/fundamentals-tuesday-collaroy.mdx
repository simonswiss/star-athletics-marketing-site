### Building confident movers through fun and play

Our **FUNdamentals** sessions are designed for younger athletes who are developing the building blocks for lifelong participation in sport and physical activity.

**Athletes will develop:**

* Running, jumping and throwing fundamentals
* Balance, coordination and agility
* Confidence in movement and sport
* Physical literacy through games and challenges
* Social skills and teamwork in a supportive environment

These sessions focus on enjoyment, participation and skill development while fostering a lifelong love of movement.

**Suitable for:** Typically ages 5-8.
