# CROSS COUNTRY Pop-Up Clinics #3

Round 3 is here! 🏃‍♂️🌿

Our Pop-Up Cross Country Clinics are back on the Northern Beaches to help our athletes prepare for the Sydney North and Polding carnivals, and also anyone who is racing in the NSW Athletics XC season and would like some additional focused training.

✔️ Pay-as-you-go flexibility\
✔️ Wednesday mornings and/or Friday afternoons\
✔️ Continue developing pacing skills, speed and stamina, build confidence, and prepare for the demands of higher level racing in a supportive and motivating environment.

Choose from:&#x20;

**WEDNESDAY Morning Clinics** **6:45–7:45am** at **Collaroy Plateau Park**: 27th May, 3rd June

**FRIDAY Afternoon Clinics 4-5pm** at **Progress Park Warriewood:** 29th May, 5th June

Athletes will develop key cross country skills including **pacing, cornering, passing, starts, finishes, and race-day preparation**.
