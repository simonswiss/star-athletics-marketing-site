# TERM 2 CROSS COUNTRY Pop-Up Clinics

Round 2 is here! 🏃‍♂️🌿

Our Pop-Up Cross Country Clinics are back on the Northern Beaches — and you’re invited to join us!

✔️ Pay-as-you-go flexibility\
✔️ Wednesday mornings and/or Friday afternoons\
✔️ Perfect for building endurance, strength, and confidence on varied terrain

Whether you’re training for your school or zone cross country, or just want to level up your running, these sessions are for you.

Spots are limited — come run with us!

15 May - Progress Park Warriewoo&#x64;**.**

Athletes will develop key cross country skills including **pacing, hill running, cornering, passing, starts, finishes, and race-day preparation**.

**About the Coach – Cooper Horley** Cooper is a Level 1 Youth Coach with extensive experience in both track and cross country. He is a NSW Representative Cross Country athlete, state-level track competitor, and an Australian representative in Orienteering. With many years of racing experience, Cooper brings practical insights from both his successes and setbacks, helping young athletes learn how to race smarter and more confidently. He has been coaching youth athletes on the Northern Beaches since 2021 and is passionate about developing strong foundations and a love of running.
