# TERM 1 CROSS COUNTRY Pop-Up Clinics

**6-Week Cross Country Clinic Program**\
Join us for a focused 6-week cross country clinic program designed to build confident, race-ready runners.&#x20;

Sessions run **Wednesdays**. Choose from:&#x20;

**Morning Clinic** **6:45–7:45am** at **Collaroy Plateau Park**.

**Afternoon Clinic 4-5pm** at **North Narrabeen Reserve.**

Athletes will develop key cross country skills including **pacing, hill running, cornering, passing, starts, finishes, and race-day preparation**. Time trials will be used to track progress and help athletes learn how to race smarter and stronger.

Perfect for athletes looking to improve performance and confidence ahead of the cross country season. 🏃‍♀️🏃‍♂️

**About the Coach – Cooper Horley**
Cooper is a Level 1 Youth Coach with extensive experience in both track and cross country. He is a NSW Representative Cross Country athlete, state-level track competitor, and an Australian representative in Orienteering. With many years of racing experience, Cooper brings practical insights from both his successes and setbacks, helping young athletes learn how to race smarter and more confidently. He has been coaching youth athletes on the Northern Beaches since 2019 and is passionate about developing strong foundations and a love of running.
