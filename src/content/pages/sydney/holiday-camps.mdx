### ***Program outline:***

**Unleash Your Speed at the Speed Stars Half-Day Clinic!**

Looking to take your sprinting, agility, and game performance to the next level? Our **Speed Stars Half-Day Clinic** is designed for young athletes eager to improve their speed, technique, and overall athleticism in a fun, supportive environment.

Over 3 action-packed hours, participants will:

* Master explosive **starts and acceleration**
* Develop **agility and change of direction skills**
* Play fast-paced **games that build speed for sport**
* Learn techniques to **maximize power and performance**

  Perfect for aspiring sprinters and young athletes who want to move faster, think sharper, and have a blast doing it!

**Spaces are limited**, so book your spot today and become a Speed Star!

Suitable for ages 7-14.
