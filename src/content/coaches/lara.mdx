Lara is a highly capable sprinter whose strength, talent, and intelligence shine both on and off the track. Known for her patient and thoughtful approach, she brings a calm confidence to training and competition, making her a positive influence within the Star Athletics community. Lara is not only physically strong but also smart in how she trains, understands her body, and applies feedback to continue improving.

This season, Lara has achieved a personal best — a reflection of her consistency, commitment, and willingness to trust the process. She values long-term development over short-term results and understands that sustainable performance is built through enjoyment, connection, and confidence.

Lara truly encompasses everything Star Athletics stands for. She prioritises enjoyment of the sport, meaningful connections with teammates and coaches, and a supportive environment where athletes feel safe to grow. By putting fun and belonging first, Lara demonstrates how strong performances naturally follow when athletes feel empowered and engaged. She is a role model for younger athletes, showing that success in athletics comes from balance, patience, and a genuine love for the sport.
