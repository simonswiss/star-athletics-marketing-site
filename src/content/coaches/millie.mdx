Millie is a vibrant and dedicated coach whose passion, positivity, and natural leadership shine in the Star Athletics community. Known for her encouraging approach and ability to bring out the best in others, she creates an uplifting environment where athletes of all abilities feel motivated to challenge themselves and thrive.&#x20;

As both an active athlete and a coach, Millie leads by example. She trains diligently with the senior Star Athletics squad, continuously refining her own skills.  By balancing her own athletic pursuits with her coaching responsibilities, Millie brings fresh, firsthand insights into every session she leads. Whether she is helping a runner refine their form or celebrating a teammate's breakthrough, Millie prioritises building meaningful connections and ensuring that every session is as fun as it is effective. She is a fantastic role model who inspires our athletes to embrace the process, stay consistent, and love every step of their running journey.
