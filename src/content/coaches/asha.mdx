Asha is a highly capable and well-rounded assistant coach with a strong sporting background across multiple sports. Naturally athletic and versatile, she has developed a deep understanding of movement and performance, which allows her to connect technical detail with practical application. Through her own athletic journey, Asha has worked diligently on refining her technique to become smooth, efficient, and flowing — a process that has led to consistent personal bests over time.

Along the way, Asha has navigated injuries, technical readjustments, and the mental challenges that come with long-term athlete development. These experiences have shaped her into a thoughtful and empathetic coach, giving her firsthand insight into the hurdles runners face. She is able to clearly identify technical breakdowns, explain corrections in a calm and simple way, and support athletes through change with patience and reassurance.

Asha brings a strong growth mindset to her coaching, encouraging athletes — particularly younger runners — to see challenges as opportunities for improvement. Her calm presence, attention to detail, and ability to translate her own learning into practical coaching cues make her a valuable mentor who helps athletes build confidence, resilience, and sustainable progress.
