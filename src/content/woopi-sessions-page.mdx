**Run, grow, and belong with Star-Athletics – where Mid North Coast kids train smart, build strength, and support each other.**

In just a few years, Star-Athletics has created an inclusive and encouraging space where young athletes push their limits, celebrate progress, and succeed together.

Led by Heather, sessions mix speed, strength, agility, and endurance—tailored to each persons goals, whether that’s school carnivals, team sports, or general fitness.

**At Star-Athletics, everyone knows that there is room for us all at the top.**&#x20;
